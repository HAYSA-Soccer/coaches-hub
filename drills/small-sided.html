Small-Sided Games

4v4 End Zone Game

Age Group: U8–U14

Duration: 12 minutes

Equipment: Cones, pinnies, balls

Description: Teams score by dribbling or passing into an end zone.

Coaching Points:

  • Quick transitions
  • Spread out to create space
  • Support the ball carrier
