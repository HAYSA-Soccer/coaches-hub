Passing & Receiving

Wall Pass Combination

Age Group: U10–U14

Duration: 12 minutes

Equipment: 4 cones, 1 ball

Description: Players work in pairs to practice give‑and‑go combinations.

Coaching Points:

  • Pass and move immediately
  • Receive on the back foot
  • Communication between players
