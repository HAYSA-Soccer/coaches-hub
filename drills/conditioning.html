Conditioning with the Ball

Dribble Box Fitness

Age Group: U8–U14

Duration: 10 minutes

Equipment: Cones, balls

Description: Players dribble at high intensity inside a grid with ball control challenges.

Coaching Points:

  • Keep the ball close
  • Use both feet
  • Change speed and direction
