Shooting & Finishing

1v1 to Goal

Age Group: U8–U14

Duration: 10 minutes

Equipment: Cones, balls, small goals

Description: Attacker dribbles at speed and tries to finish past a defender.

Coaching Points:

  • Explosive first touch
  • Head up before shooting
  • Accuracy over power
