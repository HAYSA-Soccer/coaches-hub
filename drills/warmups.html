Triangle Passing Warmup

Age Group: U8–U14

Duration: 10 minutes

Equipment: 3 cones, 1 ball

Description: Players pass and follow their pass around a triangle...

Coaching Points:

  • Open body shape
  • First touch away from pressure
  • Accurate passing
