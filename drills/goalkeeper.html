Goalkeeper Drills

Basic Handling Warmup

Age Group: U8–U14

Duration: 8 minutes

Equipment: Balls

Description: Keeper works on catching, scooping, and parrying.

Coaching Points:

  • Hands in “W” shape
  • Step into the ball
  • Eyes on the ball
