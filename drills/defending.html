Defending

Pressure–Cover–Balance

Age Group: U10–U14

Duration: 15 minutes

Equipment: Cones, balls

Description: Three defenders work together to pressure the ball and support each other.

Coaching Points:

  • First defender closes space quickly
  • Second defender provides cover
  • Third defender balances the shape
