Game Reschedule Center
How the Reschedule Process Works

Follow the steps below to coordinate with the opposing coach, request a field hold, and submit the official SSSL Game Change Form. Holds are manually entered into the Master Calendar and reviewed internally by the appropriate board members.
1
Contact Opposing Coach
Agree on a new date/time. Save screenshots of your conversation.
Request Contact Info
2
Check Field Availability
Use the calendar to find an open slot.
Jump to Calendar
3
Request a Field Hold
Email the scheduling team. The hold will be manually added to the Master Calendar.
Request Hold
4
Submit SSSL Form
Attach your agreement screenshots and submit the official form to SSSL.
Download Form
5
Track Your Request
View the status of your request as it moves through internal review.
Track Status
