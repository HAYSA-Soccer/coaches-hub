Game Change Process

Game Reschedule Center

This page turns the South Shore Soccer League (SSSL) game change process into a clear, step‑by‑step workflow. Coaches can see exactly where they are in the process and what needs to happen next.

Important: All reschedules must follow SSSL rules and use the official Game Change Request Form. This hub keeps everything organized and easy to track.

Step‑by‑Step Workflow

1
Coach Coordination
Contact the opposing coach, discuss conflicts, and agree on a new date, time, and location. Screenshots of text messages or email threads are acceptable proof of agreement.
2
Confirm Field & Referee Availability (Home Games)
For home games, confirm that the field and referees are available at the proposed time. Coordinate with the Referee Coordinator and check the field calendar.
3
Attach Conversation Proof
Save your conversation with the opposing coach (email thread, text message screenshots, or other written confirmation). This must clearly show both sides agreeing to the new game details.
4
Submit Official Game Change Request
Complete the SSSL Game Change Request Form and submit it along with your conversation proof. The form and email must include all required recipients (SSSL, HAYSA Board, opposing coach, opposing town president).
5
Approval, Updates & Notification
Wait for confirmation from SSSL. Once approved, SSSL updates their site and HAYSA updates TeamSideline. Families and coaches will see the new game details in their schedules.
