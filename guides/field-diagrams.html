Field Diagrams

Below are simplified diagrams for common HAYSA field sizes.

U8 Field

Approx. 30x20 yards, small goals, no penalty area.

U10 Field

Approx. 60x40 yards, build-out line, small penalty area.

U12 Field

Approx. 80x50 yards, full penalty area.

U14+ Field

Full-size field (100–120 x 60–80 yards).
