Concussion Protocol

Recognize symptoms early and follow proper return‑to‑play steps.

Signs of a Concussion

  • Dizziness or confusion
  • Headache
  • Nausea
  • Balance issues
  • Memory problems
Rule #1: When in doubt, sit them out.

Immediate Steps

  • Remove the player from activity
  • Notify parents immediately
  • Recommend medical evaluation

Return‑to‑Play

A player must be cleared by a medical professional before returning to soccer activities.
