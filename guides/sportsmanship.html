Sportsmanship & Culture

Building a positive environment for players, families, and coaches.

Modeling Behavior

Players mirror the behavior of their coaches. Stay calm, respectful, and encouraging.

Parent Expectations

  • Cheer positively
  • No coaching from the sidelines
  • Respect referees
Reminder: The referee is a partner in development, not an opponent.

Team Culture

Celebrate effort, improvement, and teamwork. Create an environment where players feel safe to try, fail, and grow.
