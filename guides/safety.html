Safety Guidelines

Keep players safe, prepared, and protected during all activities.

Hydration

Encourage players to bring water and take frequent breaks, especially in warm weather.

Weather Safety

  • Stop play immediately if lightning is seen or thunder is heard.
  • Use common sense in extreme heat or cold.
Important: Coaches must always have a phone available for emergencies.

Equipment Checks

Before each session, ensure:

  • Goals are anchored
  • Fields are free of hazards
  • Players wear shin guards
