Safety Guidelines

  • Ensure players wear shin guards at all times
  • Check the field for hazards before each session
  • Use age-appropriate balls and equipment
  • Monitor hydration — frequent water breaks
  • Stop play immediately for any head injury
  • Follow weather safety rules (lightning = immediate stop)

Safety is the responsibility of every coach. When in doubt, err on the side of caution.
