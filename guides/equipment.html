Equipment Checklist

Everything coaches and players need for safe, effective sessions.

Coach Essentials

  • Cones (12–20)
  • Whistle
  • Training pinnies
  • Extra soccer balls
  • First aid kit

Player Essentials

  • Shin guards
  • Proper footwear
  • Water bottle
  • Weather‑appropriate clothing
Tip: Keep a small “spare gear” bag with extra shin guards and water bottles.

Field Setup

  • Anchored goals
  • Clear playing surface
  • Boundary markers
